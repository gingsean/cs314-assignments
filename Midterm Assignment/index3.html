SOCIAL JUSTICE
Gonzaga University Specialized Recreation

I am one of the coordinators for a program at Gonzaga University that is called Gonzaga University Specialized Recreation (GUSR). We create an inclusive environment that involves college students and adults with disabilities. There are two separate parts to our program. The first part is sports where we do bowling with the Special Olympics in the Fall and play Basketball in the Spring. For the second part of our program we put on a play performance for the Spokane community at the end of each semester.
